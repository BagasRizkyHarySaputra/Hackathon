Help Center

Frequently asked questions and support resources.

How does the skin scan work?
LICIN uses AI-powered image analysis to detect skin conditions like acne, blackheads, whiteheads, and dark spots. Simply take a clear photo of your face in good lighting, and our algorithm will analyze your skin within seconds.
Is my skin data secure?
Yes. All photos and skin analysis data are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal skin data with third parties. You can delete your data at any time from the Privacy settings.
How accurate is the skin analysis?
Our AI model has been trained on thousands of dermatological images and achieves high accuracy for common skin conditions. However, it is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a dermatologist for serious skin concerns.
Can I customize my skincare routine?
Yes! Go to your Morning Routine or Night Routine tabs to view and customize your skincare steps. LICIN provides personalized recommendations based on your skin type and analysis results.
How do I track my skin progress?
Use the Skin Diary feature on the Home page to take weekly photos and track your skin health over time. The Progress section shows before/after comparisons and improvement percentages for each skin condition.
